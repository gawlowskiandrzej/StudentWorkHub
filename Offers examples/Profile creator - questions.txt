Questions for the profile builder when you first log in:

Please indicate the city in which you would like to work? (select with city and country)
Please select the jobs you are interested in (many choose options)
Please select the industries in which you would like to work (many choose options)
Please indicate your preferred salary range (2 number fields)
Would you like to work in the city you currently live in or in another city? (checkbox)
What mode of work do you prefer (remote, stationary, hybrid) - several to choose from
Are the perks offered by the company important to you? (categories e.g. health, sports, general)
Please indicate the working hours that suit you (2 number fields)
Are you in urgent need of work? (checkbox)
What form of contract do you prefer (contract of employment, contract of mandate, B2B, contract for specific work, fixed-term contract)?

... possible questions for our portal, e.g. when you prefer to receive new job offers (e-mail message)
